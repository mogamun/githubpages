## 1. Information We Collect

**"일기써영" (ilgi-sseoyeong) does not collect any personal information.** There is no user registration, login system, or account creation. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.

The following data is stored **exclusively on your device** and is never transmitted to external servers:

| Data | Storage | Purpose | Retention |
|------|---------|---------|-----------|
| Diary content (text) | Device local storage | Diary writing & management | Until user deletion |
| Vocabulary data | Device local storage | Learning word management | Until user deletion |
| Learning statistics (level, XP) | Device local storage | Learning motivation | Until user deletion |
| AI prompt/response history (up to 200) | Device local storage | AI feature quality | Auto-deleted after 200 entries |
| Backup snapshots (up to 10) | Device IndexedDB | Data recovery | Until user deletion |

## 2. How We Use Information

All data stored on your device is used solely to provide the app's core features:

- Diary writing and management
- AI-powered English correction (processed on-device)
- Vocabulary management
- Learning statistics and gamification

## 3. Third-Party Sharing

**We do not share any personal information with third parties.** All data is processed entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers, analytics services, or advertising networks.

## 4. Data Processing (Outsourcing)

We do not outsource any data processing to third parties.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

- **Right to access:** View your stored data within the app at any time.
- **Right to deletion:** Delete individual data through the app's settings, or delete all data by uninstalling the app.
- **Right to stop processing:** Stop using the app at any time. All processing ceases immediately.

Since the app does not require account registration, all data management is performed directly on your device.

## 6. Data Destruction

When you delete data within the app or uninstall the app, all stored data is permanently destroyed. Data stored in localStorage and IndexedDB is automatically removed by the operating system upon app uninstallation.

## 7. Security Measures

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

- **Physical measures:** All user data is stored exclusively on your device. No external server storage.
- **Technical measures:** Data transmission to external servers is architecturally blocked. Network communication occurs only during AI model downloads.
- **Administrative measures:** The app is designed to not collect or process personal information.

## 8. Cookies and Tracking

This app does not use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, or any other automatic data collection technologies.

## 9. Behavioral Information

This app does not collect, use, or provide behavioral information. No analytics, advertising, or tracking features are included.

## 10. AI Processing

All AI processing (English correction, word extraction) is performed **entirely on-device** using the Gemma model. Your diary content is never sent to external AI services or cloud servers.
